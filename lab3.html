About Web Accessibility

Web accessibility ensures that websites, tools, and technologies are designed and developed so that people with disabilities can use them. This webpage exhibits a strong commitment to accessibility, ensuring that all users, regardless of their abilities, can interact with its content efficiently. The inclusion of a dropdown skip navigation at the top allows users to quickly jump to the main content or directly to the data table, bypassing repetitive navigation links. Both the form and the data table have been made accessible: the form employs labels associated with their respective input fields, facilitating clear narration by screen readers, and the table utilizes semantic tags like , , and , ensuring that its structure and data are comprehensible to assistive technologies. The use of aria-hidden attributes on Google Material icons guarantees that decorative elements are not announced to screen readers, preventing potential confusion. Lastly, the Roboto font from Google Fonts not only provides aesthetic appeal but also ensures legibility, further enhancing the webpage's accessibility.
